長期ホテル滞在ライフ適性セルフチェック（リタイア向け）

Instructions

Please rate each statement from 1 to 5 based on how true it feels for you.
1 = Not true at all, 5 = Completely true.
Answer all 25 questions, then press “Calculate Score.”

説明

1 = 全く当てはまらない、5 = とても当てはまる。
